Search and Destroy

Objectives:
    Attacking Team:
     - Destroy one of two defending team's bases/nodes
     
    Defending Team: 
     - Kill all attacking team
     - Prevent attackers from destroying a node within round timelimit
     - Defuse their bomb

Rules:
    Two teams. Blue team is defenders. Red team is attackers.
    Two nodes are spawned as blue.
    Each team spawns next to their teammates (like CTF/CQ).
    [?] Attacking team has to wait X seconds before they can move?
    [?] The two nodes are chosen at random.
    Round has a timelimit of 2 minutes.
    Teams must win 6 rounds to win the match.
    Teams switch every two rounds.
